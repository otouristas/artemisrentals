## Day 1 — Arrive and settle

Ferry into Kamares, collect keys (Kamares pickup on request or Apollonia office), swim at Kamares or rest, dinner in Apollonia.

## Day 2 — South coast beaches

Platis Gialos morning, Chrissopigi late afternoon light, return to Artemonas/Apollonia for the evening walk.

## Day 3 — Kastro and Vathi

Morning in Kastro's lanes, afternoon bay time in Vathi, then pack for tomorrow's ferry without rushing the fuel/return step.

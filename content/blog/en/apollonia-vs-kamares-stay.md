## Apollonia

Choose Apollonia if you want evening energy, restaurants, shops, and a central launch point for daily drives to Platis Gialos, Vathi, Faros, or Kastro. Artemis’ office is here, so pickup and questions are easy.

## Kamares

Choose Kamares if you love beach-first mornings and the shortest walk after the ferry. You will still want wheels for midday beach hops inland or along the south coast.

## With a rental, both work

Ask Artemis for Apollonia pickup by default, or **Kamares on request** when your boat arrives late. Browse [where to stay](/en/sifnos-guide/where-to-stay) and [request dates](/en/book) once your hotel is set.

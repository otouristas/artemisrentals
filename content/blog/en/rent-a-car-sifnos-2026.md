Sifnos rewards travellers who can move on their own schedule. A rental car turns scattered beaches, pottery stops, and sunset dinners in Apollonia into one coherent trip.

## What you will pay

Artemis publishes indicative seasonal daily rates — compact cars start lower in spring/October and rise toward July–August. Treat the rate table as a planning baseline, then ask for your exact dates.

## How booking works

No prepayment is required to enquire. Send dates, preferred model (or ask for a suggestion), and whether you need Kamares pickup. The team confirms availability personally.

## Who should book early

July and August automatics go first. If you need an automatic Peugeot 208 or Micra, message early.

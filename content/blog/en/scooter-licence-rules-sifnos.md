Before you picture coastal scooter days, confirm you meet Artemis rental terms: drivers aged 23–75 with a licence held for more than one year.

## Which scooter class

Artemis offers SYM Symphony models from 125cc to 200cc plus Fiddle and JET4 125 options. Choose engine size based on hills, passenger load, and your experience. Not only the daily rate.

## Road manners

Wear a helmet, avoid night rides on day one, and do not treat island roads like a closed track. The goal is beach time, not adrenaline.

## EU and non-EU licences

EU-format licences covering motorcycles or AM/A1 categories are generally accepted for 125cc-class scooters. Non-EU visitors should carry an International Driving Permit alongside their home licence if it is not in English or Greek, Artemis staff verify at pickup in **Apollonia** or **Kamares on request**.

If your licence covers cars only, you cannot rent a 125cc scooter in Greece. When in doubt, message a photo of your licence before you fly, saves disappointment at the desk.

## Choosing cc for two riders

125cc SYM Symphony models handle solo riders and light hills well. With a passenger, consider 150cc or 200cc for Apollonia-to-Vathi climbs without straining the engine. JET4 and Fiddle 125 options suit confident riders on shorter hops between Kamares and Platis Gialos.

Couples splitting riding days can pair one scooter day with one car day from the same Artemis enquiry, **no prepayment** to ask.

## What makes Sifnos different

Sifnos balances quiet villages, serious food culture, pottery traditions, and beaches that feel worth the drive. Distances are short on a map, but bus frequency and luggage make a rental the practical default for most stays of three nights or more.

## How many days do you need?

Four to five days covers the classic loop: Apollonia and Artemonas, Kastro, at least two beach days, and a pottery or hiking afternoon. Two days works for a taste; a week lets you slow down.

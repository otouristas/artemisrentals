## Apollonia in practice

Use Apollonia for dinner, supplies, and evening walks along the main pedestrian spine. If you rent a car, ask where overnight parking is least stressful — alleys are narrow and summer evenings get busy.

## Artemonas

Artemonas is ideal when you want calmer nights but still want to walk down to Apollonia. The two villages form one continuous hill town experience.

## Pairing with a rental

Keeping the car near Apollonia makes beach day trips simple: leave after breakfast, return before the island's evening rhythm starts.

## The simple route

Most travellers reach Sifnos via Athens: land at ATH, transfer to the port of Piraeus, then board a ferry to **Kamares**, the island’s main harbour.

High-speed boats can take roughly **2.5–3.5 hours**; conventional ferries often take closer to **4–5 hours**. Schedules change by season — always check live departures before you lock rental times.

## After you dock

From Kamares it is a short drive up to Apollonia, where Artemis Rental is based. If your boat arrives late or you have heavy luggage, ask for **Kamares pickup** when you enquire.

## Island hopping into Sifnos

Ferries also connect Sifnos with other Cyclades (for example Paros, Serifos, Milos, Syros). If Sifnos is one stop in a wider route, plan the ferry chain first, then reserve Artemis wheels only for the Sifnos days.

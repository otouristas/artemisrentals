## The simple route (most travellers)

1. **Fly to Athens (ATH)**
2. **Transfer to Piraeus port** (metro, bus, taxi, or private transfer, allow buffer for luggage and traffic)
3. **Ferry Piraeus → Sifnos (Kamares)**
4. **Drive or transfer up to Apollonia** (about 5–6 km inland) and collect Artemis keys, or request **Kamares car pickup**

## Ferry duration & vessel types

- **High-speed / faster services:** often roughly **2.5–3.5 hours**
- **Conventional ferries:** often closer to **4–5 hours**, sometimes with vehicle decks useful if you bring a car from the mainland (most holidaymakers rent on-island instead)

Schedules swing hard between winter and August. Always confirm the **return sailing** before you lock non-refundable hotels.

Live Athens–Sifnos options: [Discover Cyclades ferry page](https://discovercyclades.gr/en/ferry-routes/direct/athens-piraeus-to-sifnos).

## Piraeus practicalities

- Know your **gate / quay** early, Piraeus is large.
- Build **90+ minutes** between an international landing and a tight high-speed departure if you still need to cross the city.
- Summer weekends: ports and ferries crowd, travel light onto the boat if you will rent on Sifnos anyway.

## Arriving into Kamares

Kamares is both port and beach village. ATMs, cafés, and taxis may be available when boats land, but in peak weeks pre-arranged pickup is calmer. Artemis can meet **car** clients at Kamares on request; **scooters** are collected in Apollonia.

Full port notes: [Kamares port guide](/en/sifnos-guide/kamares-port).

## Island hopping into Sifnos

Ferries also link Sifnos with other Cyclades (examples by season: Serifos, Paros, Milos, Syros and more). If Sifnos is one stop in a multi-island trip:

1. Lock the **ferry chain** first
2. Reserve Artemis wheels **only for Sifnos nights**
3. Use [Touristas AI on Discover Cyclades](https://discovercyclades.gr/en/touristas-ai/chat?island=sifnos) for ferry-aware planning

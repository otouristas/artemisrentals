## Timing beats everything

In peak summer the difference between a calm Platis Gialos morning and a stressful noon is about **90 minutes**. Leave Apollonia early; take long lunches in the shade; swim again after 16:00.

## Wind (meltemi)

Northerlies are common in July–August. If whitecaps show on open coasts, pivot to **Vathi**, **Kamares**, or sheltered Faros pockets. Scooter riders should treat crosswinds seriously on exposed ridges.

## Parking manners

- Never block chapels, bins, or hotel gates
- Fold mirrors in tight lanes
- On dirt finales (e.g. toward Vroulidia), park without ripping soft verges

## Heat & hydration

Island asphalt radiates. Keep water in the car, never leave children/pets in parked vehicles, and prefer morning hikes.

## Scooter-specific

Helmets on, no racing the bus, watch gravel near beaches, and avoid two-up overloads. If unsure, switch to a car mid-holiday, message Artemis.

## Security basics

Hide bags, skip glove-box theatres of wealth, and take passports to dinner in busy centres if your hotel safe is limited.

## Documents & deposits

Bring driving licence (and international permit if your nationality needs it), and read our [rental terms](/en/terms) before arrival.

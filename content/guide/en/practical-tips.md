## First-day checklist

- Confirm pickup point (Apollonia vs Kamares)
- Photograph existing marks with the Artemis team
- Learn the return time and fuel expectation
- Save the office number in your phone

## Peak vs shoulder

July–August means more traffic near popular beaches. May–June and September often feel like the sweet spot for driving pleasure.

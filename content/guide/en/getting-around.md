## Car strengths

- Heat, wind, and sudden weather changes feel easier
- Better for Platis Gialos beach kit days
- Automatic options available at Artemis when you want less gear work

## Scooter strengths

- Parking near busy spots is often simpler
- Lower daily cost for solo / couple trips
- Fun on short hops if you already ride

## Safety baseline

Helmets matter. Night rides on unfamiliar roads are the most common visitor mistake — leave buffer time before sunset on your first day.

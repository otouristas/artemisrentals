## Beaches worth the drive

Platis Gialos, Vathi, Faros, and quieter coves reward travellers with their own wheels. Morning starts beat midday parking pressure in July and August.

## Villages & viewpoints

Wander Apollonia and Artemonas at dusk, explore medieval **Kastro**, and watch the light change over the Aegean from coastal chapels.

## Chrysopigi

The iconic monastery headland is one of Sifnos’ signature sights — easier with a scooter or car than by infrequent buses.

## Food & pottery

Sifnos is famous for chickpea dishes, mastelo, and ceramics. Join a workshop or cooking experience when you can, then drive to the next beach with leftovers and a full tank.

## Hiking

Trail segments connect villages and coasts. Leave valuables secured and plan a vehicle drop-off/pickup if you only want to walk one way.

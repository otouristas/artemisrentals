## Platis Gialos

Long beach with tavernas and easier logistics for families. A car is more comfortable if you carry beach gear.

## Vathi

A photogenic bay with a calmer feel. Great scooter day if luggage stays at the hotel.

## Chrissopigi

Come for the monastery and rocky coastal swim spots. Combine with a slow coastal drive rather than a rushed checklist stop.

## Parking reality

Summer parking fills near popular coves. Arrive earlier than the Instagram crowd, or visit a second beach after 16:00 when others leave.
